Features

Everything you need to stay on top of your hours

Whether you're at your desk or on the go, TapShift keeps your working time accurate and effortless.

⚡

One-Tap Tracking

Tap a button to start a timer, tap again to stop. No forms, no friction. Your hours are logged instantly — even without an internet connection.

📱

Mobile & Desktop

A polished mobile experience first — swipe, tap, done. But the full web app is equally at home on a 27" monitor. One account, all your devices.

⚖️

Automatic Break Deduction

German law requires 30 min for shifts ≥ 6 h and 45 min for shifts ≥ 9 h. TapShift applies these rules automatically so you never have to think about them.

🗓️

Public Holiday Awareness

Set your state and TapShift knows which days are public holidays. Due hours are adjusted automatically — no manual corrections needed.

📊

Real-Time Balance

See your overtime or deficit at a glance — daily, monthly, and yearly. The running balance updates every minute while a timer is active.

🤒

Special Day Types

Log sick days, child-sick days, public holidays, business trips, or home office separately. Each type is tracked and shown in your history.

📵

Offline-First

Everything is stored locally first and synced to the backend when you're back online. No data loss, no waiting for a network connection.

🍎

iOS Support (Coming Soon)

A native iOS app with lock-screen Live Activities and Home Screen widgets is on the roadmap — start a timer without even opening your phone.

🎯

Custom Tracking Buttons

Create buttons for each activity — Work, Home Office, Business Trip, Deep Work, or anything you need. Assign emojis, colours, and daily goals.

Desktop & Mobile

One app. Every screen.

TapShift was designed mobile-first — the large touch targets, swipe-friendly interface and minimal UI mean you can log hours with your thumb while walking to a meeting. But when you're at your desk, the full web app expands gracefully into a proper dashboard with a multi-column layout, detailed statistics and a calendar overview.

One account keeps everything in sync. Start a timer on your phone, see the running total on your laptop — all in real time.
